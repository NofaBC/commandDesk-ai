# CareerPilot AI™ - Getting Started Guide

## Welcome to CareerPilot AI!

This guide will help you set up your account and get the most out of the platform in your first week.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account (5 minutes)

1. **Visit**: nofabusinessconsulting.com/careerpilot
2. **Sign Up**: Click "Start Free Trial" or "Sign Up"
3. **Enter Details**:
   - Email address
   - Password (min. 8 characters)
   - First and last name
4. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox for verification link
5. **Choose Plan**: Select Free or start a 14-day trial of a paid plan

**Tip**: Use your professional email address for easier networking connections.

---

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile (15-20 minutes)

A complete profile ensures accurate AI recommendations.

### Basic Information
- Current job title and company
- Years of experience
- Location (for salary insights)
- Industry and function

### Work History
- Add at least your last 3 positions
- Include job titles, companies, dates
- Add 2-3 bullet points per role describing your achievements
- Use the AI assistant to improve your descriptions

### Education
- Degrees, certifications, bootcamps
- Schools and graduation years
- Relevant coursework or honors

### Skills
- Add all relevant technical and soft skills
- Rate your proficiency level for each
- Let AI suggest skills you might have missed

### Career Goals
- Short-term goals (6-12 months)
- Long-term goals (3-5 years)
- Target roles or companies
- Salary expectations
- Geographic preferences

**Profile Completion Score**: Aim for 100% to unlock all features and get better recommendations.

---

## Step 3: Take Your Skills Assessment (30-45 minutes)

Understanding your current skills is crucial for growth.

1. **Navigate to**: Dashboard → Skills Assessment
2. **Select Assessment Type**:
   - General Professional Skills
   - Technical Skills (for tech roles)
   - Industry-Specific Skills
3. **Complete Questions**: Answer honestly about your experience
4. **Review Results**: 
   - Your skill levels across categories
   - How you compare to peers
   - Skills gaps for target roles
5. **Save Your Profile**: Results are used for personalized recommendations

**Tip**: Set aside uninterrupted time for the most accurate assessment.

---

## Step 4: Build or Upload Your Resume (20-30 minutes)

A strong resume is foundational to your job search.

### Option A: Upload Existing Resume
1. Go to: Dashboard → Resume Builder
2. Click "Upload Existing Resume"
3. Select your file (PDF, Word, or text)
4. AI will parse and import your content
5. Review and make any corrections

### Option B: Build from Scratch
1. Go to: Dashboard → Resume Builder
2. Click "Create New Resume"
3. Choose a template
4. Fill in each section using your profile data
5. Follow AI suggestions for improvements

### Resume Optimization
- Run ATS compatibility check
- Implement AI suggestions
- Add quantifiable achievements
- Optimize for target roles
- Review against job descriptions

**Goal**: Achieve 90%+ ATS compatibility score.

---

## Step 5: Set Up Job Alerts (10 minutes)

Get notified when relevant opportunities appear.

1. **Go to**: Dashboard → Job Search
2. **Create Saved Search**:
   - Job titles you're interested in
   - Preferred locations
   - Salary range
   - Company size/type preferences
3. **Set Alert Frequency**:
   - Real-time (as jobs are posted)
   - Daily digest
   - Weekly digest
4. **Enable Notifications**:
   - Email alerts
   - Mobile push notifications (if app installed)

**Tip**: Start with 2-3 focused searches rather than one overly broad search.

---

## Step 6: Talk to Your AI Career Coach (5 minutes)

Get personalized advice on your career situation.

1. **Click**: "Ask AI Coach" button (visible on all pages)
2. **Introduce Yourself**: Tell the AI about your current situation
3. **Ask Questions**:
   - "What should I focus on first?"
   - "How can I improve my chances of landing a [target role]?"
   - "What skills are most important for my goals?"
4. **Save Important Advice**: Bookmark useful conversations

**Example Starter Questions:**
- "I want to transition from marketing to product management. Where should I start?"
- "How do I negotiate salary for my next role?"
- "What's the best way to explain a 2-year career gap?"

---

## Step 7: Explore the Learning Hub (10 minutes)

Identify courses to close your skills gaps.

1. **Go to**: Dashboard → Learning Hub
2. **View Recommendations**: AI-suggested courses based on your goals
3. **Browse Learning Paths**: Structured tracks for specific outcomes
4. **Save Courses**: Bookmark courses to take
5. **Set Learning Goals**: Commit to completing X courses per month

**Quick Win**: Enroll in one short course (2-4 hours) to build momentum.

---

## Step 8: Practice Interviewing (15 minutes)

Build confidence before real interviews.

1. **Go to**: Dashboard → Interview Simulator
2. **Choose Practice Type**:
   - Behavioral (for most roles)
   - Technical (for tech roles)
   - Custom (specific company/role)
3. **Start Practice Session**:
   - Answer AI-generated questions
   - Get real-time feedback
   - Review areas for improvement
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your interview scores over time

**Goal**: Practice 2-3 times per week leading up to real interviews.

## Common First-Week Questions

**Q: How long until I see job matches?**
A: Job matches appear immediately after completing your profile. The more complete your profile, the better the matches.

**Q: Do I need to take the skills assessment right away?**
A: Not required, but highly recommended. It significantly improves the quality of your recommendations.

**Q: Can I change my career goals later?**
A: Yes! Update your goals anytime in Profile Settings. Your recommendations will adjust automatically.

**Q: How many applications should I submit per week?**
A: Quality over quantity. Aim for 5-10 well-targeted applications rather than 50 generic ones.

**Q: Is the mobile app required?**
A: No, the web platform has full functionality. The app is for convenience and on-the-go access.
