## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**
Yes, you can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

**What happens if I cancel?**
You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period. Your data is saved for 90 days in case you decide to return.

**Do you offer refunds for annual plans?**
Yes, we offer prorated refunds for annual plans canceled within the first 30 days.

**Can I pause my subscription?**
Not currently, but you can cancel and reactivate anytime. Your data is preserved for 90 days.

**Is there a commitment required?**
No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment required (even annual plans can be canceled with prorated refunds).
