# CareerPilot AI™ - Features Guide

## AI Career Coach

Your 24/7 intelligent career advisor powered by advanced AI.

**Capabilities:**
- Answer career-related questions in real-time
- Provide personalized advice based on your profile
- Suggest action items for career advancement
- Help with decision-making (job offers, career changes)
- Review and critique career documents

**How to use:**
1. Click the "Ask AI Coach" button in your dashboard
2. Type your question or describe your situation
3. Receive instant, personalized advice
4. Save important conversations for future reference

**Example questions:**
- "Should I accept this job offer?"
- "How do I negotiate salary?"
- "What skills should I learn for a data science role?"
- "How do I explain a gap in my resume?"

---

## Resume Builder & Optimizer

Create professional, ATS-friendly resumes with AI assistance.

**Features:**
- **Template Library**: 20+ professional templates
- **AI Optimization**: Real-time suggestions to improve content
- **ATS Compatibility**: Ensures your resume passes applicant tracking systems
- **Keyword Analysis**: Identifies missing keywords for target jobs
- **A/B Testing**: Compare multiple resume versions
- **Export Options**: PDF, Word, plain text formats

**How to create a resume:**
1. Navigate to "Resume Builder" in your dashboard
2. Choose a template or import existing resume
3. Fill in your information section by section
4. Use AI suggestions to improve each section
5. Run ATS compatibility check
6. Download in your preferred format

**AI Suggestions include:**
- Action verb recommendations
- Quantifiable achievement examples
- Industry-specific keywords
- Formatting improvements
- Length optimization

## Interview Simulator

Practice interviews with AI-powered feedback.

**Features:**
- **Behavioral Interviews**: STAR method practice
- **Technical Interviews**: Role-specific technical questions
- **Video Practice**: Record yourself and get feedback
- **Real-time Analysis**: Pace, filler words, confidence scoring
- **Question Bank**: 500+ common interview questions
- **Custom Scenarios**: Practice for specific companies/roles

**Interview Types:**
- General behavioral interviews
- Technical coding interviews (for software roles)
- Case interviews (for consulting roles)
- Presentation interviews
- Panel interviews

**How it works:**
1. Select interview type and difficulty level
2. Choose practice mode (text or video)
3. Answer AI-generated questions
4. Receive instant feedback on your responses
5. Review improvement suggestions
6. Track your progress over time

**Feedback metrics:**
- Answer completeness
- Clarity and conciseness
- Confidence level
- Use of specific examples
- Communication skills

## Skills Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your professional skillset.

**Assessment Types:**
- **Technical Skills**: Programming, tools, platforms
- **Soft Skills**: Communication, leadership, teamwork
- **Industry Knowledge**: Domain-specific expertise
- **Emerging Skills**: Future-focused capabilities

**Process:**
1. Take initial skills assessment (30-45 minutes)
2. Receive detailed skills profile
3. Compare against industry benchmarks
4. Identify skills gaps for target roles
5. Get personalized learning recommendations

**Results include:**
- Skills proficiency ratings (Beginner → Expert)
- Industry percentile rankings
- Skills in demand for your target roles
- Recommended skills to learn next
- Estimated time to proficiency
