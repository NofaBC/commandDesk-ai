# CareerPilot AI™ - Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues & Solutions

This guide covers the most common technical issues and how to resolve them.

---

## Login & Account Access

### Cannot Log In

**Symptoms**: Error message when trying to log in, or login button does nothing.

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify email address**: Ensure you're using the correct email (check for typos)
2. **Reset password**: Click "Forgot Password" and follow instructions
3. **Check email verification**: Look for verification email in inbox/spam
4. **Clear browser cache**: Try Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
5. **Try different browser**: Test in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
6. **Disable browser extensions**: Ad blockers can interfere with login
7. **Check Caps Lock**: Password is case-sensitive

**Still not working?**
Contact support@nofabusinessconsulting.com with your email address.

---

### "Email Not Verified" Error

**Symptoms**: Can't access account features, message says email not verified.

**Solutions**:
1. **Check inbox**: Look for verification email from noreply@careerpilotai.com
2. **Check spam folder**: Email may be filtered
3. **Resend verification**:
   - Go to login page
   - Click "Resend Verification Email"
   - Wait 2-3 minutes
4. **Add to safe senders**: Add noreply@careerpilotai.com to your contacts
5. **Try different email**: Some corporate emails block automated emails

**Verification email expired?**
Emails are valid for 24 hours. Request a new one if expired.

---

### Password Reset Not Working

**Symptoms**: Reset email not arriving or link doesn't work.

**Solutions**:
1. **Wait 5 minutes**: Emails can be delayed
2. **Check spam/junk folders**
3. **Use the link within 1 hour**: Reset links expire
4. **Request new reset link** if expired
5. **Try different email provider**: Some block automated emails
6. **Contact support** if you still can't reset

**Note**: You can only request 3 password resets per hour for security.

## Profile & Resume Issues

### Cannot Upload Resume

**Symptoms**: Upload fails, error message, or file not accepted.

**Solutions**:
1. **Check file format**: Must be PDF, Word (.doc/.docx), or plain text
2. **Check file size**: Must be under 5MB
3. **Remove password protection**: Encrypted files can't be processed
4. **Simplify formatting**: Complex tables/graphics can cause issues
5. **Try different format**: Convert Word to PDF or vice versa
6. **Use Chrome/Firefox**: Some browsers handle uploads better

**File too large?**
Compress images in your resume or remove graphics to reduce size.

---

### Resume Parsing Errors

**Symptoms**: Uploaded resume has missing sections or incorrect information.

**Solutions**:
1. **Review parsed content**: Manually correct any errors
2. **Use standard section headers**: "Work Experience," "Education," "Skills"
3. **Avoid unusual formatting**: Stick to common resume layouts
4. **Rebuild from scratch**: Use our resume builder for best results
5. **Upload simpler version**: Remove graphics, tables, and complex formatting

**Important**: Always review AI-parsed content for accuracy.

---

### Profile Completion Stuck at 99%

**Symptoms**: Can't reach 100% profile completion.

**Solutions**:
Check these often-missed fields:
- **Phone number**: In Contact Information
- **LinkedIn URL**: In Profile Links
- **Career goals**: Both short-term AND long-term
- **Preferred locations**: At least one location required
- **Salary expectations**: Range for target role
- **Skills proficiency**: Must rate each skill
- **Work achievements**: At least 2 bullets per position

**Tip**: Hover over profile completion bar to see what's missing.

## AI Coach & Features

### AI Coach Not Responding

**Symptoms**: Chat doesn't load, messages not sending, or no response.

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh the page**: Press F5 or Cmd+R
2. **Check internet connection**: Ensure stable connection
3. **Clear browser cache**: May fix loading issues
4. **Try different browser**: Test in incognito/private mode
5. **Disable ad blockers**: Can block AI features
6. **Check API status**: Visit nofabusinessconsulting.com/status

**Response taking long?**
During peak hours, responses may take 10-15 seconds.

---

### AI Recommendations Not Relevant

**Symptoms**: Job matches, course suggestions, or advice doesn't align with goals.

**Solutions**:
1. **Complete profile to 100%**: Incomplete profiles reduce accuracy
2. **Update career goals**: Make sure they're current and specific
3. **Rate your skills accurately**: Be honest about proficiency levels
4. **Add more work history**: More data = better recommendations
5. **Update preferences**: Check location, salary, and role preferences
6. **Give feedback**: Use thumbs up/down to train the AI

**Takes time**: AI improves as it learns from your feedback and activity.

---

### Interview Simulator Not Recording

**Symptoms**: Video recording doesn't start or crashes during practice.

**Solutions**:
1. **Allow camera/microphone access**: Check browser permissions
2. **Close other apps using camera**: Zoom, Teams, etc.
3. **Update browser**: Use latest version
4. **Try different browser**: Chrome works best for video
5. **Check device drivers**: Update camera drivers if needed
6. **Use external webcam**: If built-in camera isn't working
7. **Try text mode**: Alternative if video doesn't work

**Browser Permissions**:
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Site Settings → Camera/Microphone
- Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Permissions
- Safari: Preferences → Websites → Camera/Microphone

## Job Search & Applications

### No Job Matches Appearing

**Symptoms**: Empty job board or very few matches.

**Solutions**:
1. **Expand search criteria**: Broaden locations or salary range
2. **Add more skills**: Ensure your skills are listed
3. **Check job alert settings**: May be too restrictive
4. **Update target roles**: Make sure job titles are common
5. **Be patient**: Job database updates daily
6. **Try manual search**: Use search bar with keywords

**New users**: May take 24 hours for initial matches to appear.

---

### Application Tracker Not Updating

**Symptoms**: Applied jobs not showing or status not changing.

**Solutions**:
1. **Manually add applications**: Click "Add Application" button
2. **Refresh the page**: Press F5
3. **Check filters**: May be filtering out applied jobs
4. **Sync with email**: Connect Gmail to auto-import applications
5. **Update status manually**: Click on application to edit

**Auto-tracking only works for jobs applied through the platform.**

---

### Job Links Not Working

**Symptoms**: Clicking job leads to 404 or expired page.

**Solutions**:
1. **Job may be filled**: Listings removed when filled
2. **External link expired**: Original post may be deleted
3. **Search directly**: Google the job title and company
4. **Apply on company site**: Go directly to employer career page
5. **Set fresh job alerts**: Focus on recent postings

**Job postings expire**: Listings older than 30 days may be removed.

## Learning & Skills

### Courses Not Loading

**Symptoms**: Learning Hub blank or courses won't open.

**Solutions**:
1. **Refresh the page**: Press F5
2. **Clear browser cache**: Can fix loading issues
3. **Disable ad blockers**: May block course embeds
4. **Check external site**: Course may be temporarily unavailable
5. **Try different browser**
6. **Visit course directly**: Click "View on [Platform]" to go to source

**Note**: We curate courses from external platforms. Availability depends on the source.

---

### Skills Assessment Not Saving

**Symptoms**: Progress lost when returning to assessment.

**Solutions**:
1. **Complete in one session**: Don't close browser mid-assessment
2. **Disable browser "Do Not Track"**: Can prevent saving
3. **Allow cookies**: Required for progress saving
4. **Use desktop browser**: Mobile may have saving issues
5. **Contact support**: We can reset assessment if needed

**Tip**: Assessment takes 30-45 minutes—set aside uninterrupted time.

## Payment & Billing

### Payment Failed

**Symptoms**: Subscription not activating, payment declined.

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify card details**: Check number, expiry, CVV, zip code
2. **Check card balance**: Ensure sufficient funds
3. **Try different card**: May be issuer restriction
4. **Use PayPal instead**: Alternative payment method
5. **Contact bank**: May be flagged as fraud
6. **Disable VPN**: Can trigger payment blocks

**International cards**: Ensure your bank allows international transactions.

---

### Cannot Cancel Subscription

**Symptoms**: Cancel button not working or subscription still active.

**Solutions**:
1. **Go to correct page**: Account Settings → Subscription → Cancel
2. **Confirm cancellation**: Must click final confirmation button
3. **Wait for email**: Cancellation confirmation sent within 5 minutes
4. **Check active date**: Access continues until period end
5. **Contact support**: We can manually cancel if needed

**Refund requested?** Processing takes 5-7 business days.

---

### Charged Incorrectly

**Symptoms**: Wrong amount charged or unexpected charge.

**Solutions**:
1. **Check billing history**: Account Settings → Billing → Invoices
2. **Annual vs monthly**: Verify your billing cycle
3. **Contact support**: Email support with invoice number
4. **Request refund**: If error confirmed, refund issued within 7 days

**All charges appear as "NOFA Business Consulting" on statements.**

## Mobile App Issues

### App Won't Install

**Symptoms**: Download fails or app won't open.

**Solutions**:
1. **Check device compatibility**:
   - iOS: Requires iOS 13 or later
   - Android: Requires Android 8.0 or later
2. **Free up storage**: Need at least 200MB free
3. **Update OS**: Install latest iOS/Android version
4. **Try different network**: Use WiFi instead of cellular
5. **Restart device**: Simple but effective

---

### App Crashes or Freezes

**Symptoms**: App closes unexpectedly or becomes unresponsive.

**Solutions**:
1. **Force close and reopen**: Swipe up (iOS) or force stop (Android)
2. **Update to latest version**: Check App Store/Play Store
3. **Restart device**: Clears memory issues
4. **Reinstall app**: Delete and download again
5. **Check iOS/Android version**: May need OS update
6. **Free up storage**: Low storage causes crashes

---

### App Not Syncing with Web

**Symptoms**: Changes made on app not appearing on web, or vice versa.

**Solutions**:
1. **Force sync**: Pull down to refresh
2. **Log out and back in**: Both app and web
3. **Check internet connection**: Sync requires active connection
4. **Wait a few minutes**: Sync can take 1-2 minutes
5. **Contact support**: If sync issues persist

## Browser & Technical Issues

**Solutions**:

- Firefox 88+
