# CareerPilot AI™ - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is CareerPilot AI?
CareerPilot AI is an AI-powered career guidance platform that helps professionals make informed decisions about their career path, skill development, and job transitions. It combines intelligent coaching, resume optimization, interview practice, job matching, and learning resources in one platform.

### Who is CareerPilot AI for?
CareerPilot AI is designed for professionals at all career stages:
- Recent graduates entering the workforce
- Early-career professionals (0-5 years)
- Mid-career professionals seeking growth
- Career changers transitioning to new fields
- Senior professionals targeting executive roles
- Anyone returning to work after a break

### How does the AI work?
Our AI is trained on millions of career data points including job postings, successful resumes, career transitions, and salary data. It uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning to provide personalized recommendations based on your unique profile and goals.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for all data. Your information is never sold to third parties. Read our full privacy policy at nofabusinessconsulting.com/privacy.

### Can I apply to jobs through the platform?

## Technical & Account Issues

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

### Why can't I log in?
Common reasons:
- Incorrect email or password
- Email not verified (check your inbox)
- Account suspended (contact support)
- Browser issues (try clearing cache or different browser)

### My resume upload failed. What should I do?
Ensure your file is:
- PDF, Word (.doc/.docx), or plain text format
- Under 5MB in size
- Not password-protected

If issues persist, try a different file format or contact support.

### The AI is giving me irrelevant suggestions. Why?
This usually means your profile is incomplete. Ensure:
- Profile is 100% complete
- Skills are accurately rated
- Career goals are clearly defined
- Work history is up-to-date

### Is there a browser extension?
Not currently, but we're working on Chrome and Firefox extensions for Q2 2026.

### Can I import my LinkedIn profile?
Yes! Go to Profile Settings → Import from LinkedIn to automatically populate your work history, education, and skills.

### How do I export my data?
Go to Account Settings → Privacy → Export My Data. You'll receive a download link via email within 24 hours.
