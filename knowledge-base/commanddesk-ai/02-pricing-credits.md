CommandDesk AI™ uses a **subscription + credit system**:

- Credits are consumed when the AI performs certain actions

## Credit System Explained

Credits are used to cover the AI processing required to run CommandDesk AI™. Different actions consume different amounts of credits based on their computational complexity.

### Credit Costs by Action

| Action | Credits Used |
|--------|--------------|
| Import Knowledge Base Document | 10 credits |
| Knowledge Base Rebuild / Reindex | 50 credits |
| Standard AI Answer | 2 credits |
| Deep AI Answer (with citations) | 5 credits |
| Auto-generated KB Article Draft | 15 credits |

### Standard AI Answer (2 credits)
Quick response to customer questions using existing knowledge base content.

**Use Case**: Most customer support queries

### Deep AI Answer (5 credits)
Comprehensive response with detailed explanations and citations from multiple sources.

**Use Case**: Complex questions requiring thorough explanations

### Document Import (10 credits)
Process and index a new document into your knowledge base.

**Use Case**: Adding new help articles or documentation

### Knowledge Base Reindex (50 credits)
Complete rebuild of knowledge base search index.

**Use Case**: After major documentation updates or structural changes

### Auto-generated Article (15 credits)
AI drafts a knowledge base article based on common unanswered questions.

**Use Case**: Filling documentation gaps

## Credit Expiration Policy

### Subscription Credits
- **Reset Monthly**: Credits refresh at the start of each billing cycle
- **Do Not Roll Over**: Unused credits expire at the end of the billing period
- **Calculated Daily**: Usage is tracked per day for analytics

### Top-Up Credits
- **Never Expire**: Remain available until used
- **Used First**: System uses subscription credits before top-up credits
- **Transferable**: Move with you if you change plans

---

## What Happens When Credits Run Out

If you run out of credits:

1. **AI Responses Pause**: Automated responses will temporarily stop
2. **System Notification**: You'll receive an immediate alert
3. **Dashboard Warning**: Credit status displayed prominently
4. **Email Alert**: Notification sent to account administrator

### Resolution Options:

**Option 1: Upgrade Subscription**
- Increase your monthly credit allocation
- Takes effect immediately
- Better value for sustained high usage

**Option 2: Purchase Top-Up Credits**
- One-time credit purchase
- Credits never expire
- Good for temporary needs

**Option 3: Wait for Billing Cycle Reset**
- Free and Starter plans reset monthly
- Only viable for low-urgency situations

## Choosing the Right Plan

### Consider These Factors:

**Volume of Questions**
- How many customer questions do you receive daily?
- Multiply by 2 credits per standard answer

**Number of Products**
- Each product typically needs its own knowledge base
- Check knowledge base limits per plan

**Documentation Updates**
- Frequent updates require more document import credits
- Consider reindexing costs for major changes

**Team Size**
- Business plan required for multi-user access
- Role-based access control needs

**Support Needs**
- Standard support vs. priority support
- Onboarding assistance requirements

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

### What happens to unused subscription credits?
Subscription credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and do not roll over.

### Do top-up credits expire?
No. Top-up credits purchased separately never expire and remain available until used.

### Can I get a refund if I don't use all my credits?
Subscription fees are non-refundable, but you can cancel at any time to prevent future charges.

### How do I track my credit usage?
Your dashboard displays real-time credit usage, remaining balance, and usage history.

### Can I set up usage alerts?
Yes. You can configure alerts at 75%, 90%, and 100% credit usage thresholds.

### Is there a plan for enterprise customers?
Yes. Contact sales for custom enterprise plans with higher limits and dedicated support.

### Can I buy credits in advance?
Yes. Purchase top-up credits at any time. They never expire and are used after subscription credits.
